ÔøΩÔøΩ Alert Types

1. Percentage Change Alert

Get notified when a token's price moves up or down by a specific percentage.

/subscribe BTC 5 up
This will alert you when Bitcoin price increases by 5% from the current price.

2. Exact Price Alert

Get notified when a token reaches a specific price.

/subscribe BTC at 45000
This will alert you when Bitcoin reaches exactly $45,000.

3. Price Level Alert

Get notified when a token goes above or below a specific price.

/subscribe BTC above 45000
This will alert you when Bitcoin price goes above $45,000.
